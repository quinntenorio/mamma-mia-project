About Us

Mamma Mia can be found in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, in Colorful Colorado. Stop by after spending a satisfying day exploring the beautiful city of Golden, the Clear Creek river, and the many hiking trails surrounding the area.


Our Beginning...

Mamma Mia started out as a dream that turned into a reality. Four people from four very different backgrounds came together over the love of one thing: Italian food. They wanted to bring the authencity of real Italian food to the midwest. They dreamed of opening a restaurant that would have the food and atmosphere that they had long desired to see in the midwest. And from that dream, Mamma Mia was born.

We are proud to offer a mix of authentic Italian food and some popular American varieties of the food that so many people love. We also have a selection of wine that includes some of our favorite brands, both made in the U.S. and from France and Italy.

Today...

We are doing what we love: serving good food to amazing people. As of today, Mamma Mia has won the award for Best Restaurant in Golden for 5 consecutive years! We are proud to hold that tile and hope to hold it for many years to come. We hope that you chose to dine with us and become part of our family!
